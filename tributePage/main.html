Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose

A man who played a vital role in freedom of India

Here's a time line of Netaji's life:

  • 1897 - Born in Cuttack, India.
  • 1909 - 12-year-old Subhas Bose followed his five brothers to school.
  • 1912 - secured the second position in the matriculation examination & joined college.
  • 1921 - Decided not to take examination for the ICS & fight for Freedom of India.
  • 1923 - Bose was elected the President of All India Youth Congress and also the Secretary of Bengal State Congress.
  • 1927 - Bose became general secretary of the Congress party.
  • 1930 - Bose become Mayor of Calcutta,his hometown.
  • 1938 - Bose had become a leader of national stature and agreed to accept nomination as Congress President,but british servent MK Gandhi opposed his new position.
  • 1939 - Bose organised the All India Forward Bloc.
  • 1941 - Bose escaped from house arrest & arrived in Kabul, Afghanistan.
  • 1941 - Bose arrived in Berlin,Germany.
  • 1943 - Bose departed Germany for Tokyo, Japan via submarine.Later went to Singapore.
  • 1943 - Bose took command of the Indian National Army and assumed the leadership position of the Indian Independence League.
  • 1945 - Mysterious death(some says,he was seen in some parts of India even after).
  • Later - INA secured some parts of india and claimed Independence, although, their success was not big but historins believe ,this fire lead to independence of India in 1947.
